Adrenaline and fun in Cubatão

The Cubatão River, one of the main rivers in the Greater Florianópolis area, offers a wide variety of sections for rafting, each with different characteristics such as water speed, rapids, and rocks. There are options for adrenaline levels and fun for all tastes, whether for the most adventurous or those who prefer a gentler experience.

Rafting in Três Coroas

Experience the only rafting tour with an 8 km route through the thrilling rapids of the Paranhana River. During the tour, we'll make two exciting stops for activities, including a refreshing dip in the rapids and an amazing raft surfing session on the waves. Additionally, challenge yourself with the NEW WAVE Big Drop. All safety equipment, such as vests and helmets, is included, along with guides specialized in the activity.

Rafting in Paranhana

Longer route, more adventure, greater contact with nature. Interactive activities (raft surfing, cliff jumping, water slide). An inflatable raft, a river with clear water rapids, stunning scenery, natural obstacles, and only one path to follow: the path of Adventure!
